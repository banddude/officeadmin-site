Example: Email arrives

Here is what happens when a new email hits Mike's inbox.

1

Gmail scanner runs

The comms pipeline scanner checks Gmail for new messages. It runs on a schedule (morning scan at 7:45 AM PT, afternoon watchers at 3:00-3:45 PM).

2

Classification

Each message is classified into tiers. Tier 1 messages (routine, predictable replies) get auto-drafted and auto-sent. Tier 2 messages get a notification. Tier 3 messages need human review.

3

Drafter writes the reply

The Drafter module reads entity context, routing rules, and Mike's writing conventions. It writes a reply in Mike's voice. Up to 5 drafts run in parallel.

4

Approval or auto-send

Tier 1 drafts go out automatically at 9:30 AM. Everything else becomes an Apple Reminder for Mike to review. He approves, edits, or rejects from his phone.

5

Knowledge extraction

Key information from the conversation is extracted and stored in MemPalace. Contact details, project references, and action items get linked to the right entities in the CRM.
